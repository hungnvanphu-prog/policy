Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to Water & Medicine Reminder (package name: com.aurazone.water_medicine_reminder), provided by Aurazone Studio.

Information You Enter

The app lets you enter reminder and wellness information such as profile name, weight, daily water goals, water intake logs, medicine names, medicine schedules, dose history, and relative care records.

This reminder data is stored locally on your device. Aurazone Studio does not sell this information and does not use it for advertising.

Analytics And Crash Reports

The app uses Google Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to help us understand app usage, diagnose crashes, and improve reliability. These services may collect technical information such as device model, operating system version, app version, crash logs, diagnostic data, approximate usage events, and identifiers used by Firebase.

We do not intentionally send your medicine names, water intake history, or relative care notes to Firebase.

Notifications

The app schedules local notifications on your device for water, medicine, and care reminders. Notification scheduling is handled on your device.

Data Sharing

Aurazone Studio does not sell your personal data. Data may be processed by service providers that help operate the app, including Google Firebase for analytics and crash reporting.

Data Export, Backup, And Deletion

You can export, back up, restore, or delete app data from the app's Settings screen. Uninstalling the app may also remove locally stored app data, depending on your device and backup settings.

Children

The app is intended for personal reminder use and is not directed to children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, contact us so we can help address the issue.

Medical Disclaimer

Water & Medicine Reminder is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always follow instructions from qualified healthcare professionals.

Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
